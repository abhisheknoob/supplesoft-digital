DIFFERENT TYPES OF PPC ADS

Every business is different, and so are its advertising needs. The right paid ad strategy isn’t one-size-fits-all—it depends on your company’s size, location, and what you offer your customers. Whether you’re a local startup looking for visibility or an established brand aiming for national reach, choosing the right type of paid ads is key to maximizing results. Our tailored approach ensures your campaigns align with your business goals, attract the right audience, and deliver measurable ROI

  • Shopping Ads

    • showcase your products directly in search results with images, prices, and store details. They attract high-intent buyers and drive more qualified traffic to your online store.

  • YouTube Ads

    • Allow businesses to reach and engage audiences through video campaigns on the world’s largest video platform. They’re perfect for boosting brand awareness, storytelling, and driving targeted traffic with measurable results.

  • Search Ads

    • With Search Ads, advertisers select the keywords they want to target. When users search for those terms, Google displays tailored ad copies created by the advertiser. You only pay when someone clicks on your ad, making it a highly targeted and cost-effective strategy.

  • Performance Ads

    • Google Performance Ads use AI-driven targeting and automation to deliver ads across Search, Display, YouTube, and more. They maximize conversions by optimizing bids, creatives, and placements in real time, ensuring higher ROI with less manual effort.

  • Display Ads

    • Display Ads appear in the form of text, images, or videos across Google’s vast partner network. While they typically have lower conversion rates compared to search ads, they are excellent for increasing brand visibility and building awareness among a wider audience.

  • Remarketing Ads

    • Remarketing Ads target people who have already interacted with your website or shown interest in your services. By reminding them of your offerings, these ads serve as the final nudge that often turns potential leads into paying customers.
